# Learning Track: Business Architecture

## Purpose
Develop proficiency in business capability modeling, value stream mapping, and operating model design using BIZBOK and APQC.

## Modules
- Business Architecture fundamentals (BIZBOK)
- Capability mapping and heat maps
- Value stream mapping
- Business process modeling (BPMN)
- Operating model design

## Certifications
- Business Architecture Practitioner
- BIZBOK Certified Business Architect
