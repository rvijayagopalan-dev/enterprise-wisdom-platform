# Learning Track: Governance

## Purpose
Build competency in enterprise governance, risk management, and compliance program design.

## Modules
- GRC framework fundamentals (COBIT, NIST, ISO)
- Enterprise risk management
- Compliance program design (SOX, GDPR, PCI DSS)
- AI governance and ethics
- Architecture governance
- Data governance

## Certifications
- GRC Practitioner
- AI Governance Specialist
- Risk Management Professional
