# Sub-Domain: Sustainability & ESG Forces

## Purpose
Track environmental, social, and governance obligations that are increasingly board-level priorities.

## Areas
- Carbon reduction targets
- Energy consumption monitoring
- Sustainable supply chain requirements
- ESG reporting mandates (SEC, EU CSRD)

## Impact Areas
- Data and cloud strategy (green cloud)
- Operating model changes
- Supply chain partner selection

## Outputs
- ESG Compliance Assessment
- Carbon Footprint Analysis
- Sustainable Strategy Recommendations
