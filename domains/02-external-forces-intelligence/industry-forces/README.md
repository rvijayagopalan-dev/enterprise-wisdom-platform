# Sub-Domain: Industry Forces

## Purpose
Evaluate the competitive structure of the industry using Porter's Five Forces.

## Framework: Porter's Five Forces

| Force | Key Questions |
|-------|--------------|
| Competitive Rivalry | Who are competitors? What is market share? |
| New Entrants | Are AI-native startups entering? What are barriers? |
| Supplier Power | Are vendors creating lock-in? |
| Customer Power | Is customer switching increasing? |
| Substitute Products | Is AI disrupting existing products? |

## Outputs
- Industry Competitive Assessment
- Market Concentration Analysis
- Threat of Substitution Report
