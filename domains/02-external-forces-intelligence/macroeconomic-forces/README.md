# Sub-Domain: Macroeconomic Forces

## Purpose
Track macroeconomic indicators that affect enterprise revenue forecasts, cost structures, and investment appetite.

## Data Sources
- Federal Reserve, IMF, World Bank, OECD
- Economic indicator feeds

## Factors

| Factor | Strategic Impact |
|--------|-----------------|
| Interest Rates | Mortgage, banking, retail financing, real estate |
| Inflation | Supply chain, consumer spending, operating costs |
| GDP Growth | Revenue forecasts, expansion plans |
| Unemployment | Labor costs, consumer demand |

## Outputs
- Macroeconomic Signal Report
- Revenue Forecast Adjustments
- Cost Pressure Alerts
