# Sub-Domain: Market Forces

## Purpose
Track shifts in customer behavior, demographics, and digital adoption that reshape demand.

## Dimensions
- Demographics and generational shifts
- Digital adoption curves
- Customer preferences and expectations
- Consumer sentiment and spending patterns

## Retail Example
- Online shopping growth
- Loyalty program trends
- AI shopping assistant adoption

## Outputs
- Customer Behavior Analysis
- Digital Adoption Forecast
- Market Demand Signals
