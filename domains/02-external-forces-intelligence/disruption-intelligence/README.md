# Sub-Domain: Disruption Intelligence

## Purpose
Detect emerging threats from new business models, AI-native competitors, and industry convergence before they reach critical mass.

## Watch Categories
- New business models challenging incumbents
- AI-native startups and competitors
- Industry convergence (e.g., retail + tech + logistics)
- Platform and ecosystem disruptions

## Retail Example
- Amazon's logistics and AI commerce integration
- Walmart's omnichannel and tech transformation
- AI-native commerce platforms

## Outputs
- Disruption Signal Report
- Competitive Threat Forecast
- Strategic Response Recommendations
