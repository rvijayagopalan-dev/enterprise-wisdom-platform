# Sub-Domain: Geopolitical Forces

## Purpose
Assess geopolitical risks to supply chains, technology sourcing, and market access.

## Factors
- Trade Policies
- Tariffs
- Sanctions
- Regional Conflicts
- Supply Chain Disruptions

## Impact Areas
- Suppliers and manufacturing
- Distribution networks
- Technology vendor availability
- Regional market access

## Outputs
- Geopolitical Risk Register
- Supply Chain Vulnerability Report
- Market Access Assessment
