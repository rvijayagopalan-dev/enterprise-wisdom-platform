# Sub-Domain: Wisdom Synthesis

## Purpose
Synthesize patterns from enterprise memory, industry intelligence, reasoning outputs, and simulation results into distilled organizational wisdom — actionable insights that improve future strategy and decisions.

## Synthesis Sources
- Enterprise Memory (past decisions and outcomes)
- Industry Intelligence (sector patterns and benchmarks)
- Reasoning Engine outputs
- Simulation results
- External Forces Intelligence

## Synthesis Outputs
- "What transformation approaches have consistently worked for our type of organization?"
- "What are the leading indicators of transformation failure we should monitor?"
- "What is our organization's pattern of strategy execution?"

## Learning Loop
Every completed initiative, decision, and outcome feeds back into the wisdom layer — making the platform increasingly intelligent and enterprise-specific over time.

## Outputs
- Organizational Wisdom Repository
- Executive Strategic Briefings
- Transformation Pattern Intelligence
- Continuous Intelligence Updates
