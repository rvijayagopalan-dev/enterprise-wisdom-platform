# Sub-Domain: Reasoning Engine

## Purpose
Perform multi-step, context-aware reasoning over the enterprise knowledge graph and memory to answer complex strategic questions.

## Reasoning Types
- Deductive — from known facts to logical conclusions
- Inductive — from patterns to generalized insights
- Abductive — from observations to best explanations
- Analogical — from similar past situations to current guidance

## Example Reasoning Chains
- "Given rising inflation, AI adoption acceleration, and our capability gaps in supply chain, what should our strategic priorities be for next 18 months?"
- "Why have similar retail transformations failed at the capability investment stage?"

## Technology
- LangGraph — stateful multi-step reasoning
- GraphRAG — knowledge graph-augmented reasoning
- Chain-of-thought prompting over enterprise context

## Outputs
- Reasoned Recommendations with full rationale chains
- Evidence-backed strategic conclusions
- Confidence-scored insights
