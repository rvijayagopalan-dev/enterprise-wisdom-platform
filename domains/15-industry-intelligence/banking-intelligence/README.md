# Sub-Domain: Banking Intelligence

## Purpose
Track transformation patterns, AI adoption, and regulatory evolution in the banking and financial services sector.

## Tracked Areas
- Digital banking and neobank disruption
- AI in risk management and fraud detection
- Regulatory technology (RegTech) adoption
- Core banking modernization patterns
- Open banking and API ecosystem evolution

## Key Competitors Tracked
- JPMorgan Chase — AI investment and fintech strategy
- Capital One — technology-first banking model
- Goldman Sachs — Marcus digital bank evolution

## Outputs
- Banking Industry Dashboard
- Regulatory Change Impact Reports
- Banking Transformation Playbook
