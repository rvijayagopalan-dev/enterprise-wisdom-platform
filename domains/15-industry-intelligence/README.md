# Domain 15: Industry Intelligence

## Purpose
Maintain living intelligence on industry players, patterns, and best practices — continuously updated so the platform can generate industry-contextualized strategy and architecture recommendations.

## The Distinction from Case Studies
Case studies are static. This domain maintains **living intelligence** that is continuously refreshed from real-world signals.

## Sub-Domains

| Sub-Domain | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| [Retail Intelligence](./retail-intelligence/README.md) | Retail industry patterns, benchmarks, and competitors |
| [Banking Intelligence](./banking-intelligence/README.md) | Banking and financial services intelligence |
| [Cross-Industry Patterns](./cross-industry-patterns/README.md) | Universal transformation patterns across industries |

## Tracked Organizations
- **Retail**: Amazon, Walmart, Costco, Target
- **Banking**: JPMorgan, Capital One, Goldman Sachs

## Intelligence Sources
- Public earnings calls and analyst reports
- Industry analyst publications (Gartner, Forrester)
- Technology partner case studies
- Enterprise transformation post-mortems

## Outputs
- Industry Trend Reports
- Competitor Strategy Analysis
- Industry Benchmark Comparisons
- Transformation Pattern Library
