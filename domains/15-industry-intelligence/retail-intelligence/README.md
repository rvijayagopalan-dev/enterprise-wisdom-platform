# Sub-Domain: Retail Intelligence

## Purpose
Maintain continuously updated intelligence on retail industry dynamics, competitor strategies, and transformation patterns.

## Tracked Areas
- Omnichannel and digital commerce evolution
- AI adoption in retail (personalization, forecasting, checkout)
- Supply chain transformation patterns
- Workforce automation trends
- Loyalty and customer experience innovation

## Key Competitors Tracked
- Amazon — logistics, AI commerce, AWS
- Walmart — omnichannel, tech transformation
- Costco — membership model, supply chain
- Target — digital and store integration

## Outputs
- Retail Industry Dashboard
- Competitor Strategy Reports
- Retail Transformation Playbook
