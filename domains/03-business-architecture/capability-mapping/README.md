# Sub-Domain: Capability Mapping

## Purpose
Identify, organize, and assess the business capabilities required to execute strategy.

## Capabilities
- Level 1/2/3 capability decomposition
- Capability heat maps (performance, investment, gap)
- Capability-to-strategy traceability
- Capability maturity assessment

## Outputs
- Business Capability Map
- Capability Heat Map
- Capability Gap Analysis
- Capability Investment Roadmap
