# Domain 03: Business Architecture

## Purpose
Map, analyze, and evolve enterprise capabilities, value streams, and business processes to align with strategic objectives.

## Sub-Domains

| Sub-Domain | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| [Capability Mapping](./capability-mapping/README.md) | Define and assess business capabilities |
| [Value Streams](./value-streams/README.md) | Model end-to-end value delivery flows |
| [Process Models](./process-models/README.md) | Business process modeling and optimization |
| [Business Information Models](./business-information-models/README.md) | Data and information structures supporting business |

## Frameworks
- **BIZBOK** — Business Architecture Body of Knowledge
- **APQC** — Process Classification Framework
- **BPMN** — Business Process Model and Notation

## Outputs
- Capability Heat Maps
- Capability Roadmaps
- Business Transformation Models
- Value Stream Diagrams
