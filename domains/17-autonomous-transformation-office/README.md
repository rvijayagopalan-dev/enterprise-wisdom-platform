# Domain 17: Autonomous Transformation Office

## Purpose
Move from AI-assisted recommendations to AI-driven execution — automatically generating roadmaps, architecture artifacts, and governance reviews that would otherwise require a full transformation office and consulting team.

## The Shift
```
Current State: AI Recommends → Humans Execute
Future State:  AI Recommends → AI Plans → AI Executes → AI Monitors → AI Corrects
```

## Sub-Domains

| Sub-Domain | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| [Roadmap Automation](./roadmap-automation/README.md) | Auto-generate transformation roadmaps from strategy |
| [Artifact Generation](./artifact-generation/README.md) | Automatically produce architecture and governance artifacts |
| [Governance Automation](./governance-automation/README.md) | Automate governance reviews, compliance checks, and approvals |

## Automated Deliverables
- Jira Epics and Stories from strategic initiatives
- Architecture diagrams from requirements
- Governance review packages
- Risk assessments
- Compliance evidence packages
- Progress and status reports

## Outputs
- Auto-Generated Transformation Roadmaps
- AI-Produced Architecture Artifacts
- Automated Governance Reviews
- Continuous Monitoring Dashboards
