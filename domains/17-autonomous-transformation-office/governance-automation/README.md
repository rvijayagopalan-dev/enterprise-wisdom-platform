# Sub-Domain: Governance Automation

## Purpose
Automate routine governance activities — reviews, compliance checks, risk assessments, and approvals — to reduce overhead while maintaining control.

## Automated Activities
- Architecture review board (ARB) pre-screening
- Compliance gap detection against standards
- Risk scoring automation
- Benefits realization reporting
- Portfolio health checks

## Human Escalation
Automation handles routine checks. Complex or high-risk decisions escalate to human reviewers with pre-packaged analysis.

## Outputs
- Automated Governance Review Reports
- Compliance Posture Alerts
- Portfolio Health Dashboards
- Escalation Packages for Human Review
