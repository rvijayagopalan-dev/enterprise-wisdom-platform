# Sub-Domain: Risk Management

## Purpose
Identify, assess, and mitigate risks across strategic, technology, cyber, and AI dimensions.

## Risk Categories
- **Strategic Risk** — market, competitive, and execution risks
- **Technology Risk** — system failures, obsolescence, integration
- **Cyber Risk** — security threats, data breaches, ransomware
- **AI Risk** — model failures, bias, hallucinations, misuse

## Outputs
- Enterprise Risk Register
- Risk Heat Maps
- Mitigation Plans
- Risk Monitoring Dashboard
