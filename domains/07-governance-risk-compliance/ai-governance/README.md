# Sub-Domain: AI Governance (GRC)

## Purpose
Provide oversight, accountability, and risk controls for AI deployments to satisfy regulatory and ethical requirements.

## Controls
- AI risk classification (EU AI Act tiers)
- Bias and fairness assessments
- Explainability documentation
- Human override mechanisms
- AI incident logging and response

## Outputs
- AI Risk Register
- AI Compliance Map (EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF)
- AI Ethics Policy
- AI Audit Trail
