# Domain 07: Governance, Risk & Compliance

## Purpose
Establish enterprise-wide governance frameworks, risk management practices, and compliance programs across all domains.

## Sub-Domains

| Sub-Domain | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| [Portfolio Governance](./portfolio-governance/README.md) | Oversight of initiative and investment portfolios |
| [Architecture Governance](./architecture-governance/README.md) | Standards, review boards, technology compliance |
| [Data Governance](./data-governance/README.md) | Data quality, ownership, lineage, and compliance |
| [AI Governance](./ai-governance/README.md) | AI risk, ethics, regulatory compliance, oversight |
| [Risk Management](./risk-management/README.md) | Strategic, technology, cyber, and AI risk |
| [Compliance](./compliance/README.md) | SOX, PCI DSS, GDPR, HIPAA, ISO standards |

## Frameworks
- **COBIT** — IT governance and management framework
- **NIST CSF** — Cybersecurity framework
- **ISO 27001** — Information security management
- **ISO 42001** — AI management system

## Compliance Standards
SOX · PCI DSS · GDPR · CCPA · HIPAA · ISO 27001 · ISO 42001

## Outputs
- Governance Frameworks
- Risk Registers
- Compliance Maps
- Audit Evidence Packages
