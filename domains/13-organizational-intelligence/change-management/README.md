# Sub-Domain: Change Management

## Purpose
Design and execute structured change programs that move people through awareness, desire, and adoption.

## Frameworks Applied
- ADKAR — individual change journey
- Kotter's 8 Steps — organizational change program
- Bridges Transition Model — managing the transition period

## Program Components
- Change impact assessment
- Change readiness assessment
- Communication planning
- Training and capability building
- Resistance management
- Reinforcement and sustainability planning

## Outputs
- Change Management Plan
- Change Readiness Report
- Communication Calendar
- Resistance Management Plan
