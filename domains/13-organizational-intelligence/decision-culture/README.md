# Sub-Domain: Decision Culture

## Purpose
Assess and evolve how decisions are made across the organization — speed, quality, accountability, and bias.

## Assessment Areas
- Decision-making speed and effectiveness
- Decision rights clarity (RACI analysis)
- Common decision biases (behavioral economics lens)
- Data-driven vs. intuition-driven decision patterns
- Risk tolerance culture

## Connection to Behavioral Economics
- CFO optimizes for risk reduction
- CTO optimizes for scalability
- CEO optimizes for growth

The platform surfaces these biases to help frame proposals appropriately.

## Outputs
- Decision Culture Assessment
- Decision Rights Map (RACI)
- Bias Awareness Report
- Decision Quality Improvement Plan
