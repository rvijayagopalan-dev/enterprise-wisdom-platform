# Domain 13: Organizational Intelligence

## Purpose
Analyze human factors — culture, politics, incentives, stakeholder dynamics — that determine whether transformations succeed or fail. The biggest blind spot in most enterprise platforms.

## The Core Insight
Transformations fail not because of cloud, AI, or architecture — but because of politics, incentives, culture, and resistance. This domain addresses those factors systematically.

## Sub-Domains

| Sub-Domain | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| [Stakeholder Analysis](./stakeholder-analysis/README.md) | Identify, map, and manage transformation stakeholders |
| [Organizational Network Analysis](./organizational-network-analysis/README.md) | Understand informal influence and communication networks |
| [Change Management](./change-management/README.md) | Design and execute change programs that achieve adoption |
| [Leadership Alignment](./leadership-alignment/README.md) | Align executive and leadership teams to transformation vision |
| [Decision Culture](./decision-culture/README.md) | Assess and evolve how decisions are made across the org |

## Frameworks
- **ADKAR** — Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement
- **Kotter's 8-Step Change Model**
- **McKinsey 7S** — Strategy, Structure, Systems, Shared Values, Skills, Style, Staff
- **Bridges Transition Model**

## AI Agent
**Change Advisor** — answers "Who will resist this transformation?" and provides stakeholder-specific engagement strategies.

## Outputs
- Stakeholder Engagement Plans
- Change Readiness Assessments
- Resistance Risk Analysis
- Organizational Change Roadmap
