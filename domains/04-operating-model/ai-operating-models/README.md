# Sub-Domain: AI Operating Models

## Purpose
Design operating models that govern how AI is built, deployed, monitored, and governed across the enterprise.

## Key Concepts
- AI Center of Excellence (CoE) structure
- AI development and deployment lifecycle
- Human-in-the-loop governance
- AI risk and ethics oversight
- Model monitoring and performance management

## Outputs
- AI Operating Model Blueprint
- AI CoE Design
- AI Governance Policy Framework
- AI Risk and Ethics Controls
