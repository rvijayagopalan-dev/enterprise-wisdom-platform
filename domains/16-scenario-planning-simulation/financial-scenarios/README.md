# Sub-Domain: Financial Scenarios

## Purpose
Model the financial impact of different business conditions and decisions across revenue, cost, and investment dimensions.

## Scenario Types
- Revenue growth / decline scenarios
- Cost inflation scenarios (labor, cloud, supply chain)
- Investment constraint scenarios
- Interest rate and macro impact scenarios

## Modeling Techniques
- Sensitivity analysis (which variables matter most)
- Break-even analysis
- NPV and IRR under different assumptions
- Monte Carlo simulation for probabilistic outcomes

## Outputs
- Financial Scenario Models
- Revenue Impact Forecasts
- Investment ROI Under Scenarios
- Break-Even Analysis
