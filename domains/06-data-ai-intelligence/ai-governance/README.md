# Sub-Domain: AI Governance

## Purpose
Establish oversight, accountability, and risk controls for AI systems deployed across the enterprise.

## Frameworks
- EU AI Act — risk-based AI regulation
- NIST AI RMF — AI risk management framework
- ISO 42001 — AI management system standard
- OECD AI Principles

## Governance Areas
- AI risk classification and assessment
- Bias and fairness monitoring
- Explainability and transparency requirements
- Human oversight and intervention controls
- AI incident response

## Outputs
- AI Governance Framework
- AI Risk Register
- AI Ethics Policy
- AI Compliance Map
