# Domain 01: Strategy Intelligence

## Purpose
Generate, evaluate, and continuously refine enterprise strategy by combining internal drivers with external signals.

## Sub-Domains

| Sub-Domain | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| [Strategic Planning](./strategic-planning/README.md) | Define vision, objectives, and strategic roadmaps |
| [SWOT Analysis](./swot-analysis/README.md) | Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats assessment |
| [PESTLE Analysis](./pestle-analysis/README.md) | Political, economic, social, technological, legal, environmental scan |
| [Competitive Analysis](./competitive-analysis/README.md) | Porter's Five Forces, market share, competitor positioning |
| [Industry Intelligence](./industry-intelligence/README.md) | Market trends, disruption signals, sector benchmarks |
| [Strategic Initiative Generation](./strategic-initiative-generation/README.md) | AI-generated strategic themes and initiative portfolios |

## Key Capabilities
- AI-generated strategic themes from internal + external signals
- Strategic planning frameworks (SWOT, PESTLE, Porter's Five Forces)
- Portfolio prioritization and investment theme generation
- Competitive and industry intelligence synthesis

## Outputs
- Strategic Themes
- Strategic Objectives
- Strategic Roadmaps
- Investment Priorities
- Transformation Programs

## AI Advisor
**Chief Strategy Officer Advisor** — monitors economy, markets, competition, and regulations to recommend strategic themes, investment areas, and transformation initiatives.
