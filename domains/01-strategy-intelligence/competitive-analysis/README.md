# Sub-Domain: Competitive Analysis

## Purpose
Understand competitive dynamics and positioning using structured frameworks.

## Frameworks
- Porter's Five Forces
- Market share analysis
- Competitor capability benchmarking

## Porter's Five Forces Coverage
- Competitive Rivalry — market share, pricing, differentiation
- New Entrants — barriers to entry, AI-native startups
- Supplier Power — vendor concentration, lock-in risk
- Customer Power — switching costs, customer concentration
- Substitute Products — AI disruption, alternative solutions

## Outputs
- Competitive Positioning Report
- Market Share Analysis
- Threat Assessment
- Strategic Differentiation Recommendations
