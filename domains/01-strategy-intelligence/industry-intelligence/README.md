# Sub-Domain: Industry Intelligence

## Purpose
Monitor and synthesize real-time industry trends, disruption signals, and sector benchmarks.

## Data Sources
- Gartner, Forrester, IDC
- Industry analyst reports
- Public company filings and earnings
- Open source and research communities (arXiv)

## Coverage
- Market trends and growth signals
- Industry-specific disruption patterns
- Sector benchmarks and maturity models
- Emerging technology adoption curves

## Outputs
- Industry Trend Reports
- Disruption Signals
- Sector Benchmark Comparisons
- Technology Adoption Forecasts
