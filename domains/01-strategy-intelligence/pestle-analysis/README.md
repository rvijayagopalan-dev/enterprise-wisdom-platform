# Sub-Domain: PESTLE Analysis

## Purpose
Evaluate macro-environmental factors that shape enterprise strategy.

## Dimensions
- **Political** — government policies, trade agreements, political stability
- **Economic** — GDP, inflation, interest rates, employment
- **Social** — demographics, cultural trends, consumer behavior
- **Technological** — AI, cloud, cybersecurity, digital disruption
- **Legal** — regulatory changes, compliance requirements
- **Environmental** — ESG obligations, sustainability, climate impact

## Outputs
- PESTLE Assessment Report
- Strategic Risk Flags
- Opportunity Signals
