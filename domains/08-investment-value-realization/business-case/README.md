# Sub-Domain: Business Case

## Purpose
Generate AI-powered business cases with financial models, risk assessments, and strategic alignment justification.

## Business Case Components
- Executive summary and strategic rationale
- Problem statement and opportunity sizing
- Solution options and recommendation
- Financial model (costs, benefits, ROI, NPV, IRR)
- Risk assessment
- Implementation approach and timeline

## Outputs
- Business Case Document
- Financial Model (investment, savings, revenue, ROI, NPV)
- Risk-Adjusted Scenarios
